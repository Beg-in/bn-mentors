Our Story

We are a group of hackers who believed in an idea and came together to build it along side Bloomington Normal’s tech companies and startup community. We had fun developing a custom web app to help others and that was reward enough. Our hope is that the community will respond and it’s leaders will create office hours to give of themselves and help our community be stick and strong!

Our Values

Other focused

We are completely focused on others. Selfish motives are lame and don’t fit here.

Giving

We give without thought of what we might give back. It's all about helping others.

Humility

Domain expertise doesn’t put anyone above anyone else. We put ourselves beneath those we serve.

Stewardship

This isn’t anyone’s job but it’s important and we can do it, so we believe that means we should.

Our Vision

A stronger, more open and connected Bloomington Normal where today’s leaders hold office hours to help tomorrow’s lead here.
